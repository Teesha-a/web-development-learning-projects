THE BEST MOVIES I HAVE EVER WATCHED

My 3 fav movies

3 Idiots

It is a fun movie about 3 best friends studying together in an engineering college and dealing with their dean V.I.R.U.S. who respects marks more than knowledge.

Tare Zameen Par

It is a heart-touching movie about a dyslexic kid and how noone understands his difficulties till he finally finds a painting teacher suffering from the same.

Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani

A movie where the class topper girl goes on a trip with 3 bestfriends and gets a new outlook on life and have fun in every moment.
